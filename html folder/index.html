    About FTMS

    Farmer Transport Management System (FTMS) is a web-based platform designed to support farmers in managing transportation of their agricultural products. In many rural areas, farmers face difficulties in finding reliable and affordable transport services to deliver their crops to markets on time. This system aims to solve that problem by connecting farmers directly with transport drivers. FTMS allows farmers to easily create transport requests by entering details such as pickup location, destination, type of crop, load, and distance. Based on this information, the system can also provide an estimated transport cost, helping farmers make better decisions. The platform includes two main user roles: farmers and drivers. Farmers can book transport services and track the status of their requests, while drivers can view available requests and accept or complete them. This improves communication and coordination between both parties. The system reduces dependency on middlemen and helps in minimizing delays in crop delivery. It also helps farmers save time, effort, and transportation costs. By digitizing the transport process, FTMS improves efficiency and transparency in the agricultural supply chain.

    FTMS is designed with a simple and user-friendly interface so that even users with basic technical knowledge can use it easily. The system ensures smooth operation and better accessibility for rural users.

    In the future, FTMS can be enhanced by adding features like real-time tracking, online payment options, and live market price updates. These improvements will make the system more powerful and beneficial for farmers.

    Overall, Farmer Transport Management System is a smart solution that helps farmers transport their crops efficiently, reduces losses, and supports the growth of the agricultural sector.
